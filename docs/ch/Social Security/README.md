1st Pillar – State Social Insurance (Mandatory)

Goal: Cover basic living needs
Financed by: Payroll contributions + federal/cantonal funds
Main schemes:

- AHV/AVS – Old-age & survivors’ pensions
- IV/AI – Disability insurance
- EL/PC – Supplementary benefits (if AHV/IV insufficient)
- EO/APG – Income compensation (military service, maternity)
- ALV/AC – Unemployment insurance

Applies to all residents and workers.

2nd Pillar – Occupational Pension (Mandatory for employees)

Goal: Maintain previous standard of living
Financed by: Employer + employee contributions
Scheme:

- BVG/LPP – Occupational pension fund

Mandatory for employees earning above a legal threshold; optional for self-employed.

3rd Pillar – Private Pension (Voluntary)

Goal: Individual retirement planning and tax optimization
Types:

- Pillar 3a – Tax-privileged, contribution limits, restricted access
- Pillar 3b – Flexible savings, no tax privilege

Health & Accident Insurance (Outside the Pillars)

- Health insurance (LAMal/KVG) – Mandatory, private insurers, basic package regulated
- Accident insurance (LAA/UVG) – Mandatory for employees, employer-paid
